Our Story

The vadiMAP solution was created in 2018 to fill a clear void in the market.

With 25 years of experience in the energy industry, our experts wanted to innovate in the energy transition industry by offering a new option, backed by artificial intelligence in order to offer a reliable and more accessible alternative to companies to increase the energy performance or their buildings.

Let's build a greener and more resilient future together.
